About Rankr

Rankr is our project for our Engineering Design course at Texas Tech University. The goal of this project was to learn to design like an engineer. We started with coming up with thie idea to make this site and then interviewed our fellow students as well as some gamers that we personally knew but didn't document. The insight we gathered from these interviews was then used to create a paper wireframe of the site, which can be found by _____ (shhh, dont tell anyone). We then created a mockup on figma, the link to the prototype is also held where the wireframe picture is (again, our secret). This all culminated to the real development of this site, where we are glad to sponsor a community of gamers both casual and hardcore alike to share their opinions on games and find new ones to play. We hope you enjoy using our site as much as we enjoyed making it!
