— ENGR-1340 / Texas Tech

Built to rank the games that matter.

Rankr started as a classroom brief: design something real, for a real audience, using engineering-design methods. We interviewed gamers, drew paper wireframes, built a Figma prototype, and shipped this — a game-focused site to explore titles, find highly rated games, and share opinions.

— The Process

How We Got Here

— Step 01
Interview

Spoke with 14 gamers and classmates to understand what they actually wanted out of a game-focused platform.

— Step 02
Wireframe

Paper wireframes first. Every screen sketched before a single pixel was pushed.

— Step 03
Figma

High-fidelity prototype to pressure-test the flow — sort, search, browse, rate.

— Step 04
Ship

Built with plain HTML/CSS/JS and a small Java server talking to the IGDB API.
